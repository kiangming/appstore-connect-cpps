Soft-delete flow: sync flags DB items absent from Google’s latest (successful) response as “Deleted on Google” rather than hard-deleting them — the amber banner surfaces the anomaly for multi-operator awareness, flagged rows sort to the bottom in red (reusing the divergence-first language), the filter chip collapses them for everyday use, and removal is an explicit per-row (or bulk) acknowledge with a light confirm. Nothing is deleted without a human clicking Confirm.
